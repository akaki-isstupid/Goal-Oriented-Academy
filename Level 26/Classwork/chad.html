About Chad

Chad is a landlocked country in north-central Africa. It is bordered by Libya to the north, Sudan to the east, the Central African Republic to the south, Cameroon and Nigeria to the southwest, and Niger to the west.

Culture

The culture of Chad is rich and diverse, influenced by the various ethnic groups. Traditional music, dance, and art are integral parts of Chadian culture.

Geography

Chad has a variety of geographical features including deserts, mountains, and savannahs. The country is home to the Sahara Desert in the north and Lake Chad in the southwest.

Economy

Chad's economy is primarily based on agriculture and livestock. It also has oil reserves, which have become a significant part of its economy.
